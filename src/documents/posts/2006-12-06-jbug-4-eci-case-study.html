Developing Advanced network management system built on top of Jboss’ JEE infrastracture
ECI Broadband Access Division is currently developing its Next-Generation Network Management System that controls, monitors and troubleshoot, the ECI-based broadband-access infrastructure at leading Telco providers. ECI development is based on J2EE architecture and the JBoss application server in its center. In this lecture, we will describe the network management functions provided by the management system and will discuss some technical consideration within the context of Java/J2EE/JBoss architecture.
