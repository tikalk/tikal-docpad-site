JBoss 5, currently still in early beta4, is an evolutionary version: joining the IoC trend with its rewritten microcontainer-based core and introducing many new concepts and improved implementations such as Virtual Deployment Framework, OSGi classloaders, JAXWS-2.0 and JAXB-2.0 implementations and more. In this session we'll review these features and others and try to analyze some of the long-term objectives behind them..
