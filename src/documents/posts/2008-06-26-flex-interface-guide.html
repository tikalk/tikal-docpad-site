Flex Inetrface Guide (FIG) is a series of articles covering possibilities, techniques, and challenges designers and developers will confront when designing Flex rich Internet applications.

Specifically, the series covers:

- Planning and structuring Flex applications

- Special considerations for designing web versus desktop Flex applications

- The design of rich content displays

- Appropriate use of motion in application design

- Improving an application’s efficiency of use

- Ensuring your users feel safe using your application and trust it with their data
