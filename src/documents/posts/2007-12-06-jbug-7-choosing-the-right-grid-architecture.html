As the old saying goes: "It is easy to kill a fly with a gun" - experiencing performance and non-linear scalability issues is not always best resolved using a grid-based or space-based architecture. In this short summary session, we'll try to give a bird's-eye look on the existing technologies and solutions and try to recommend an approach to help deciding which should be employed in your project and to what extent.
